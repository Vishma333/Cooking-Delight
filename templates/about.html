Our Journey to Your Kitchen

Cooking Delight began not in a test kitchen, but in a home kitchen, filled with the aromas of freshly ground spices and the warmth of family. It started with a simple idea: to preserve the recipes of our mothers and grandmothers—the secret dals, the festive biryanis, the everyday sabzis—that have been the heart of our family for generations.

We realized that so many people yearned for the taste of authentic home-cooked Indian food but felt intimidated by the complexity. Our mission became clear: to demystify Indian cooking and make it accessible, joyful, and a part of everyday life for everyone, everywhere. Every recipe we share is a story, every book a legacy, and every meal an invitation to our table.
